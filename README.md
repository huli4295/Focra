## ⚙️ Recording Your First Video

Focra works with a simple interface. You do not need technical skills to start. 

1. **Open the App:** Click the Focra icon on your desktop.
2. **Select Area:** Choose whether to record your full screen or a specific window. 
3. **Check Audio:** Use the microphone menu to select your input device.
4. **Start Recording:** Press the large red button.
5. **Move Your Mouse:** As you move the cursor and click buttons, Focra identifies the action. 
6. **Watch the Zoom:** The software zooms in on the active area automatically.
7. **Stop Recording:** Press the stop button when you finish your task.

The recording saves automatically to your video folder. You find the file as an MP4 by default.

## 💻 Settings and Configuration

You can change how the software behaves in the settings menu. Click the gear icon to open the configuration panel.

* **Save Location:** Choose where Focra stores your finished files.
* **Video Quality:** Select between high or medium resolution. High resolution uses more space on your hard drive.
* **Frame Rate:** Set this to 30 or 60 frames per second. 60 frames per second looks smoother for fast-paced demos.
* **Microphone Gain:** Adjust the volume levels for your voice recordings.
